About This Platform

This free web platform helps Grade 12 students in Ethiopia practice for their national English exam. We provide a comprehensive set of practice questions, detailed explanations, and performance analytics to help students identify their strengths and weaknesses.

Our mission is to make quality exam preparation accessible to all Ethiopian students, regardless of their location or economic background. The platform is continuously updated with new questions and features based on the latest exam patterns.
